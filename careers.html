Join Our Team
L&C Grounds Company is seeking professional, detail oriented candidates to join our team in Sterling, VA. We are a busy, fast-paced grounds company seeking energetic and hard-working candidates.
Lawn & Landscape Foreman/Crew Leaders
Requirements:
  • Must be at least 18 years of age or older
  • Landscape experience required
  • Possess a valid driver's license, clean driving record, and no criminal record
  • Be able to work flexible hours up to 6 days weekly
  • Be dedicated, dependable, and willing to work in allweather conditions
  • Able to lift up to 50 lbs., stand for extended periods, bend, kneel and perform landscaping duties
  • Must be drug and alcohol free
  • Must have reliable transportation to and from site location
Primary responsibilities:
  • Weed removal and proper mulch application
  • Shrub and tree installation
  • Proper pruning techniques
  • Plant identification (turf, trees, shrubs, weeds)
  • Leaf, brush and debris removal, raking and blowing
  • Sweep walkway of debris
  • Snow removal
  • Ability to operate equipment to include mowers, string trimmers, hedge trimmers, edger's, blowers
CREW LEADERS Additional Responsibilities:
  • Possess ability to drive trucks, front loaders, and skid steers
  • Accurately complete daily timesheets
  • Ability to instruct and provide oversight, safety and directions to laborers
  • Ability to communicate clearly with management
  • Vehicle and trailer checks
