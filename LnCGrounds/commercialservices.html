Commercial Ground Services

L&C Grounds Company understands the importance of keeping your commercial property clean, attractive, and well maintained to make a good first impression for your customers.  We offer these commercial services in a dependable, efficient, and high-quality manner helping our clients achieve their goals.


Sweeping Services (Shopping centers, office parks, HOAs, etc.)

Making a good first impression is critical to catching the eye of new customers and keeping them as repeat customers.  Our team believes that this starts with top notch curb appeal from the property entrance through the exterior. The property is cleared of debris, free of trash and any additional small dirt partials.  We are available 24 hours a day, 365 days a year to meet your service needs.  Contact us to set up an appointment to see how we can exceed your expectations.


Commercial Grounds Maintenance (Lawn & Landscaping)

In addition to ensuring that your lot is clean and free of debris, we provide practical and affordable landscape management services to light up your property and keep it looking presentable throughout the year.

 

We offer these landscape maintenance services:

  • Turf care
  • Tree, Shrub and Ornamental Maintenance
  • Landscape Design & Enhancements
  • Annual Plant Rotations
  • Holiday Decorations
  • Snow & Ice Management

Contact us today to set up an appointment to discover how L&C Grounds Company will create a custom landscape plan to address your property’s specific needs.


Snow & Ice Management

L&C Grounds Company offers comprehensive Snow & Ice Management services catered toward meeting your specific winter maintenance requirements. Get ahead of the storm by contacting our team today to begin planning the most effective and efficient way of caring for your property during the winter months.
