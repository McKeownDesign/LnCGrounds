What We Do

Lucas and Clark (L&C) Grounds Company strives to leave every frontier more beautiful than when we arrive, while pioneering ways of providing the utmost customer service possible.  Serving the Northern Virginia and Washington D.C. area, L&C Grounds Company provides property maintenance and landscape services to its customers.  L&C Grounds Company is properly licensed and insured for protection of our clients and employees. We are here to serve both your commercial and residential needs in any way we can.
